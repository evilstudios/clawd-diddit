## Discovery Questions for the Call

### Business Context
1. What's your biggest operational bottleneck right now?
2. How many customer service inquiries do you handle per day/week?
3. What % of inquiries are repetitive/automatable?
4. Do you have existing AI/automation in place?
5. What's the business impact of faster response times? (revenue, retention, NPS)

### Technical Context
6. What systems do you use? (CRM, dispatch software, communication tools)
7. Do you have a technical team, or would we be the AI technical partner?
8. Any data we can integrate with? (historical support tickets, delivery data, etc.)

### Budget & Timeline
9. What's your budget range for AI implementation?
10. What's your timeline? (Pilot in 30 days? Full rollout in 6 months?)
11. How do you measure success? (cost savings, revenue growth, efficiency metrics)
