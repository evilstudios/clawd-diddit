## 6-Month Roadmap

### Month 1-2: **Foundation** ($11K → $14K)
**Focus:** Fix product, optimize listings, establish baseline
- Fix P0 bugs (login + purchase)
- ASO audit & optimization
- AppLovin baseline analysis
- **Target: +$3K MRR**

### Month 3-4: **Scale** ($14K → $19K)
**Focus:** Profitable acquisition, organic growth
- Scale AppLovin (maintain ROAS >1.5x)
- Organic installs from ASO
- Facebook reactivation
- **Target: +$5K MRR**

### Month 5-6: **Accelerate** ($19K → $25K)
**Focus:** Pour gas on winners
- AppLovin aggressive scale
- Growth experiments
- International expansion
- **Target: +$6K MRR**
