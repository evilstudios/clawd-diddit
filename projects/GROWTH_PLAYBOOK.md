# Growth Playbook: 0 → $5K MRR Per Product

## Framework: The Revenue Equation
`MRR = Traffic × Conversion Rate × Average Plan Price`

To reach $5K MRR, we need to optimize all three variables for each product.

## Evil Apples: Gaming Revenue Optimization

### Current State Analysis Needed:
- [ ] Current DAU/MAU
- [ ] Current monetization methods (ads? IAP? subscription?)
- [ ] Average session time
- [ ] Retention curves

### Revenue Streams:
1. **In-App Purchases**
   - Premium card packs ($1.99-4.99)
   - Cosmetic items (avatars, themes)
   - "Remove ads" ($2.99 one-time or $0.99/month)
   
2. **Subscription Model**
   - Evil Apples Premium: $4.99/month or $39.99/year
   - Benefits: Ad-free, exclusive cards, early access to new decks, custom game modes
   - **Target: 1,000 subscribers = $4,990 MRR**

3. **Ad Revenue**
   - Interstitial ads between games
   - Rewarded video ads (watch ad for free card pack)

4. **Physical Products**
   - Already have card game on Amazon
   - Add merchandise (shirts, mugs) with better margins

### Growth Tactics:
- **Viral Loop:** "Share your funniest play" feature → social sharing
- **Referral Program:** Invite 3 friends → get premium deck free
- **Tournaments:** Weekly competitions with prizes
- **Content Marketing:** Blog about funniest plays, card combinations
- **TikTok/Instagram:** Short-form content showing hilarious gameplay
- **App Store Optimization:** A/B test screenshots, description
- **Push Notifications:** "Your friends are playing now!"

## Universal Growth Strategies (All Products)

### 1. Email Marketing
- Collect emails aggressively
- Nurture sequences for each product
- Win-back campaigns for churned users

### 2. Referral Programs
- Give 1 month free for each referral
- Referred user gets discount too (double-sided)

### 3. Annual Plans
- Offer 2 months free on annual (increase LTV, reduce churn)

### 4. Retargeting Ads
- Facebook/Instagram pixel on all sites
- Retarget visitors who didn't convert

### 5. Review/Social Proof
- Automate requests for 5-star reviews
- Display testimonials prominently
- Case studies for each product

### 6. Pricing Experiments
- A/B test pricing tiers
- Add "Pro" tier above current highest
- Decoy pricing (middle tier looks like best value)
