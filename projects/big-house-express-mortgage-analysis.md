- Principal portion: ~$1,300/month
- **Required minimum payment:** Likely $700-800/month

### ✅ Option 2: Loan Recast (if lump sum available)
- Make lump-sum principal payment
- Lender recalculates monthly payment over remaining term
- **Cost:** $200-500 fee
- **Benefit:** Keeps 3.5% rate, lowers payment, still done in 2034
- **Requirement:** Cash on hand + lender must offer recasting

### ✅ Option 3: HELOC for Short-Term Cash Flow
- Open Home Equity Line of Credit at ~8-9%
- Use sparingly for temporary cash flow needs
- Pay off when cash flow improves
- **Benefit:** Preserves 3.5% mortgage rate
- **Risk:** Variable rate, easy to overuse
