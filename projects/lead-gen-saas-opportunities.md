# Lead Generation System - Top Local Service Opportunities

## Selection Criteria
**Best candidates have:**
- High transaction value ($5k-50k+ per customer)
- Clear, targetable decision-maker
- Predictable buying triggers
- Low competition for B2B outreach
- Willing to pay $500-2k/month for qualified leads

## Why Cosmetic Surgeons Are #1

**The math:**
- Average facelift: $15,000
- Average breast augmentation: $8,000
- Average procedure value: ~$10,000
- Conversion rate from qualified lead: 5-10%

**If you deliver 50 leads/month:**
- 3-5 convert = $30k-50k in procedure revenue
- Your $1,500/month fee = 3-5% of generated revenue
- **That's an insane ROI for them**

**Plus:**
- They're already used to paying $200-500 per lead via Google Ads
- Your system would deliver warmer, more qualified leads for less
- High profit margins on procedures = room to pay for lead gen

## Implementation for Cosmetic Surgeons

### Lead Sources:
1. **Instagram/Facebook scraping:**
   - People engaging with cosmetic surgery content
   - Followers of competitor med spas
   - Users in target demographic (age, location, interests)

2. **RealSelf scraping:**
   - Users researching specific procedures
   - People asking questions or leaving reviews
   - Geographic targeting

3. **Yelp/Google Reviews:**
   - People reviewing or engaging with med spas
   - High-intent signals

4. **Local targeting:**
   - Zip codes with $100k+ median income
   - Proximity to med spa location

### Output:
**Google Sheet columns:**
- Name
- Instagram/Facebook handle
- Email (if available)
- Phone (if available)
- Location (city/zip)
- Interest signal (what procedure they engaged with)
- Lead score (1-10 based on engagement + demographics)
- Last activity date

### Integration options:
- **Basic:** Google Sheets export ($0 extra)
- **CRM:** Push to HubSpot/Salesforce ($200/month setup + integration)
- **SMS/Email:** Auto-outreach via Instantly/Twilio ($500/month extra)

## Go-to-Market Strategy

**Phase 1: Prove it with 1-2 cosmetic surgeons**
- Offer pilot at $500/month for 3 months
