## Must-Do Activities & Sightseeing

### Museums & Culture
1. **Rijksmuseum** - Dutch masters (Rembrandt, Vermeer)
2. **Anne Frank House** - BOOK IN ADVANCE (2 months+)
3. **Van Gogh Museum** - World's largest Van Gogh collection
4. **Stedelijk Museum** - Modern art
5. **Hermitage Amsterdam** - Rotating exhibitions

### Experiences
6. **Canal cruise** - 1-hour boat tour (€15-25/person)
7. **Bike rental** - Most Dutch way to see the city
8. **Jordaan neighborhood** - Walk charming streets, galleries, cafes
9. **Albert Cuyp Market** - Street food & shopping
10. **Vondelpark** - Beautiful city park (free)

### Day Trips (Optional)
- **Zaanse Schans** - Windmills & cheese (30 min by train)
- **Keukenhof Gardens** - PERFECT TIMING! Tulips bloom late March-April
- **Haarlem** - Charming small city (15 min by train)

### Food Experiences
- **Stroopwafels** at Albert Cuyp Market
- **Dutch pancakes** (pannenkoeken)
- **Indonesian food** (rijsttafel) - colonial heritage
- **Cheese tasting** - Try Gouda, Edam
- **Bitterballen** - Dutch bar snack

### Shopping Areas
- **9 Straatjes (9 Little Streets)** - Boutique shopping
- **P.C. Hooftstraat** - Luxury brands
- **De Bijenkorf** - Department store on Dam Square
- **Bloemenmarkt** - Floating flower market (tulip bulbs to take home)

## Practical Tips

### Weather (Late March/Early April)
- **Temperature**: 8-13°C (46-55°F)
- **Conditions**: Can be rainy, bring layers
- **Pack**: Light jacket, umbrella, comfortable walking shoes

### Transportation
- **From airport**: Train to Centraal Station (15 min, €5)
- **In city**: Trams, walking, bikes
- **OV-chipkaart**: Reloadable transit card (easier than buying tickets each time)

### Money
- **Currency**: Euro (EUR)
- **Cards**: Credit cards widely accepted
- **Cash**: Some small shops/markets cash-only

### Language
- **Official**: Dutch
- **Reality**: 90%+ speak English fluently
- **Useful phrases**: "Dank je wel" (thank you), "Alstublieft" (please/you're welcome)

### Booking Priority
1. **Anne Frank House tickets** - Book 2 months in advance (opens at midnight)
2. **Flights** - Book now (2 months out)
3. **Hotel** - Book within 2 weeks
4. **Museums** - Can book 1-2 weeks before
