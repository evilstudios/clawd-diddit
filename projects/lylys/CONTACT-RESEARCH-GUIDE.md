## Method 1: YouTube Channel About Page

### Steps:
1. Go to channel URL
2. Click "About" tab
3. Look for:
   - Email address (sometimes listed)
   - "View email address" button (for business inquiries)
   - Links to website/social media

## Method 3: Social Media

### Check These Platforms:
1. **Instagram** - Often has business email in bio
2. **Twitter/X** - Check bio or pinned tweet
3. **Facebook** - Business page contact info
4. **TikTok** - Sometimes has contact info

### Search Format:
- "@truecrimegaragetv" (Instagram)
- "true crime garage podcast" (all platforms)
- Look for verified accounts

---

## Method 4: Podcast Platforms

### Apple Podcasts
1. Search for show name
2. Click show page
3. Look for website link in description
4. Check "More by [Host Name]" for their production company

### Spotify
- Similar process
- Sometimes lists management/production company
