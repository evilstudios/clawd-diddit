## Personalization Variables

**Required for every email:**
- `{{FirstName}}` - Creator's first name
- `{{ShowName}}` - Podcast/channel name
- `{{SuperfanCount}}` - Estimated superfans (calculated from engagement)
- `{{TopFanName}}` - Most engaged fan (optional, adds credibility)
- `{{RecentEpisodeTitle}}` - Latest episode (shows we did research)

**Optional (boosts conversion):**
- `{{CommentCount}}` - Top fan's comment count
- `{{SimilarShow}}` - Comparable creator in same niche
- `{{SimilarCreator}}` - Comparable creator's name

---

## Sending Strategy

**Timing:**
- Send Tue-Thu, 10am-2pm (creator timezone)
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)

**Volume:**
- Start: 10 emails/day (warm up Instantly.ai sender reputation)
- Week 2: 25 emails/day
- Week 3+: 40 emails/day (max safe volume)

**A/B Testing:**
- Test 3 subject lines per campaign
- Track open rate, reply rate, demo booked rate
- Kill underperformers after 50 sends
