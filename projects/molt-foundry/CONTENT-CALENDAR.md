## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### TikTok
- **Post time:** 6-9am, 12-3pm, 7-11pm EST
- **Frequency:** 1-2x per day
- **Respond to comments:** Within 1 hour
- **Use trending audio:** When possible

### Twitter/X
- **Post time:** 9am, 12pm, 5pm, 9pm EST
- **Frequency:** 3-5x per day (threads + replies)
- **Engage:** Reply to mentions, quote tweets
- **Retweet:** Community content

### Instagram
- **Post time:** 11am-1pm, 7-9pm EST
- **Frequency:** 1 feed post per day + Stories throughout
- **Engage:** Reply to DMs and comments
- **Stories:** Use interactive stickers (polls, questions)

### Facebook
- **Post time:** 1-4pm EST (weekdays), 12-1pm (weekends)
- **Frequency:** 1x per day
- **Engage:** Reply to comments within 2 hours
- **Groups:** Share in relevant AI/tech groups

## Emergency Content (If Needed)

### If sales slow:
- Flash discount (10% off for 24 hours)
- Exclusive bonus for next X buyers
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Founder Q&A live stream

### If going viral:
- Ride the wave (post 2-3x normal frequency)
- Respond to every comment
- Create response videos
- Engage with sharers

### If negative feedback:
- Address concerns transparently
- Show customer success stories
- Educational content about value
- Community testimonials

## Tracking & Optimization

### Daily Metrics:
- Views per platform
- Engagement rate
- Click-through to website
- Units sold (correlate with posts)

### Weekly Review:
- Top-performing content (replicate)
- Worst-performing content (avoid)
- Engagement patterns (optimize timing)
- Sales velocity (adjust urgency)

### Optimization:
- Double down on what works
- Cut what doesn't
- A/B test messaging
- Adjust scarcity urgency based on sales pace
