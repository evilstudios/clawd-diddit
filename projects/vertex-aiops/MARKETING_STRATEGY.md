## Conversion Funnel

### Stage 1: Awareness (Cold Outreach)
- Cold email sequence (3 emails over 10 days)
- LinkedIn connection + message
- Value-first approach (free audit, ROI calculator)

### Stage 2: Interest (Discovery Call)
- 30-minute qualification call
- Identify 1-2 quick wins
- Share relevant case study
- Offer AI readiness assessment (free 30-min)

### Stage 3: Decision (Assessment Proposal)
- Custom proposal for $5K assessment
- Timeline: 2 weeks start to finish
- Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- Guarantee: Identify $50K+ in savings or refund

### Stage 4: Close (Retainer Conversion)
- Present assessment findings
- Show implementation roadmap
- Offer Phase 1 implementation (quick wins)
- Retainer proposal with 3-month minimum
- Bonus: Waive month 1 if they commit during assessment
