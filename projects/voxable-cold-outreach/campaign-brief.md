## Campaign Structure (Best Practice)

### Email Sequence (5-7 emails over 14 days):

**Email 1 (Day 1):** Initial outreach - Hook with pain point
**Email 2 (Day 3):** Value prop + social proof
**Email 3 (Day 6):** Case study or specific benefit
**Email 4 (Day 9):** Breakup email / last chance
**Email 5 (Day 12):** Alternative angle / different benefit
**Email 6 (Day 14):** Final follow-up

## Local Service Provider Pain Points

### Common Problems:
1. **Missed Calls = Lost Revenue**
   - Can't answer when on job site
   - After-hours calls go to voicemail
   - Lose customers to competitors who answer

2. **Appointment Booking Chaos**
   - Phone tag with customers
   - Double bookings
   - No-shows

3. **Customer Service Overhead**
   - Same questions over and over
   - Time spent on phone vs doing actual work
   - Can't afford receptionist

4. **Professional Image**
   - Want to seem bigger than they are
   - Voicemail feels unprofessional
   - Customers expect instant response
