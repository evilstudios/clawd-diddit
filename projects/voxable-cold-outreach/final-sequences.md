## Red Flags to Watch For

### If open rate < 30%:
- Subject lines too salesy
- Sender email has low reputation
- Emails going to spam (check spam score)

### If reply rate < 3%:
- Emails too long
- Value prop unclear
- CTA too weak or confusing

### If getting negative replies (angry, "remove me"):
- List quality issue (wrong audience?)
- Messaging too aggressive
- Sending too frequently
