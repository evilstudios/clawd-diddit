# Statement of Work - Phase 1 Lead Generation Pilot

**Between:**  
Big House Express LLC ("Client")  
Super Massive Ventures Inc. ("Contractor")

**Effective Date:** [Date]  
**Term:** 3 months from start date

---

## 1. Services

Contractor will provide lead generation and outreach services for Client's 3PL/fulfillment business:

### Deliverables:
- 500-1,000 qualified eCommerce leads per month
- Email + LinkedIn outreach campaigns
- Lead enrichment and qualification
- Weekly performance reports
- Monthly strategy calls
- Response handling and meeting scheduling support

### Target Outcomes:
- 10+ qualified meetings booked in 90 days
- 2+ active opportunities in sales pipeline
- 5% meeting rate (meetings per leads contacted)

---

## 2. Investment

### Payment Structure:
- **Upfront Setup Fee:** $1,500 (due upon signing)
- **Monthly Service Fee:** $2,500/month × 3 months
- **Total Investment:** $9,000

### Payment Terms:
- Upfront fee due upon contract execution
- Monthly fees due NET 15 (15 days after invoice)
- Invoices sent on 1st of each month

### Performance Bonuses (Optional):
- 15 meetings booked: $1,000 bonus
- 20+ meetings booked: $2,000 bonus
- First deal closed during pilot: $1,000 bonus

---

## 3. Timeline

**Month 1 (Weeks 1-4):**
- Week 1: Kickoff & discovery
- Week 2: Setup & infrastructure
- Week 3-4: Campaign launch (500 leads)

**Month 2 (Weeks 5-8):**
- Scale to 1,000 leads/month
- Optimize messaging
- Add LinkedIn channel

**Month 3 (Weeks 9-12):**
- Full-scale execution
- Pipeline support
- Upgrade decision

---

## 4. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to provide:

1. **Time & Availability:**
   - 4-6 hours in Weeks 1-2 (discovery & setup)
   - 2-3 hours/week thereafter (meetings, feedback)

2. **Resources:**
   - Sales team availability for booked meetings
   - CRM access (or Contractor will provide tracking)
   - Email domain for outreach
   - Timely feedback on messaging & leads

3. **Decision-Making:**
   - Approve messaging before launch
   - Review weekly reports
   - Participate in monthly strategy calls

---

## 5. Contractor Responsibilities

Contractor agrees to deliver:

1. **Lead Generation:**
   - 500-1,000 qualified leads/month
   - Trigger-based targeting (growth signals, pain points)
   - Lead enrichment (contact info, company data)

2. **Outreach Execution:**
   - Email campaigns (design, send, monitor)
   - LinkedIn outreach (connections, messaging)
   - A/B testing and optimization
   - Response handling and qualification

3. **Reporting & Support:**
   - Weekly performance reports
   - Monthly strategy calls
   - Real-time access to results
   - Ongoing optimization

---

## 6. Success Criteria

Phase 1 is considered successful if, by end of Month 3:

✅ **10+ qualified meetings** booked with decision-makers  
✅ **2+ active opportunities** in sales pipeline  
✅ **5% meeting rate** achieved  

If targets are met, Client has option to upgrade to Tier 2 ($7,500/month) for custom software and expanded services.

If targets are not met, contract ends after Month 3 with no further obligation.

---

### Contractor Retains:
