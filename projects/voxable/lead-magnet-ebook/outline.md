### Chapter 4: The SMB AI Implementation Roadmap (1,000 words)
- **Phase 1: Audit (Week 1-2)**
  - Identify bottlenecks and manual processes
  - Calculate current cost of inefficiency
  - Prioritize by ROI potential
- **Phase 2: Pilot (Week 3-8)**
  - Start with ONE high-impact use case
  - Set success metrics (time saved, revenue generated, cost reduced)
  - Test, measure, iterate
- **Phase 3: Scale (Month 3-6)**
  - Roll out successful pilots company-wide
  - Add second and third AI implementations
  - Train team on new workflows
- **Phase 4: Optimize (Month 6+)**
  - Continuous improvement loops
  - Integrate AI into company DNA
  - Explore advanced/custom solutions
