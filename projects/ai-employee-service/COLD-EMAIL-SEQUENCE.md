## ğŸ¯ Sequence Strategy

### **Email Cadence:**
- Day 0: Problem awareness
- Day 3: Solution introduction
- Day 7: Social proof
- Day 10: Break-up (pattern interrupt)
- Day 14: Value-add (leave on good terms)

### **Key Principles:**

1. **Personalization:**
   - Use {{firstName}}, {{company}}, {{industry}}
   - Reference similar companies in their space
   - Mention specific pain points for their industry

2. **Pattern Interrupt:**
   - Email 4 (break-up) often gets the most responses
   - Gives them permission to say "not interested"
   - Or triggers FOMO ("wait, I was interested!")

3. **Value First:**
   - Email 5 gives free value even if they never reply
   - Positions you as helpful, not just salesy
   - Opens door for future conversations

4. **Low Pressure:**
   - Never guilt-trip or be pushy
   - Give easy outs ("reply 'not interested' and I'll stop")
   - Respect their time
