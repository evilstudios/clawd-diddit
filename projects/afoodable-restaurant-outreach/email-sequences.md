## Success Metrics (Benchmarks for 50 Restaurants)

### Good Performance:
- **Open Rate:** 40-50%
- **Reply Rate:** 10-15% (restaurants are warmer than B2B)
- **Positive Replies:** 5-8 restaurants interested
- **Demos Booked:** 3-5 calls
- **Expected Signups:** 2-3 restaurants

### Great Performance:
- **Open Rate:** 50-60%+
- **Reply Rate:** 15-20%
- **Positive Replies:** 8-10 restaurants
- **Demos Booked:** 5-8 calls
- **Expected Signups:** 4-6 restaurants

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

### If open rate < 30%:
- Subject lines too corporate
- Email reputation issue
- Going to spam (check mail-tester.com)

### If reply rate < 8%:
- Email too long (shorten Email 1)
- Value prop unclear (simplify math)
- CTA too weak (make more specific)

### If negative replies:
- Wrong audience (too corporate, wrong restaurant type)
- Too salesy (soften tone)
- Sending too frequently (slow down)
