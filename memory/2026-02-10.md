## Risks & Mitigation

**Risk:** Reddit bans automation  
**Mitigation:** 
- Human-like behavior (delays, rotation)
- High-quality comments only
- Aged accounts (100+ karma)
- Respect rate limits

**Risk:** Account sourcing  
**Mitigation:**
- Buy aged accounts ($5-15 each)
- Start with 10, scale to 50+

**Risk:** Proxy costs at scale  
**Mitigation:**
- Not needed until 20+ accounts
- Residential proxies: $300-500/mo
