# HEARTBEAT.md - Proactive Check-ins

## What to Check (Rotate Daily)

### Every Heartbeat:
- Check if any new files/PRs need attention
- Look for opportunities to build something useful

### Monday/Thursday:
- Review business metrics (when APIs connected)
- Check for any alerts or anomalies
- Suggest optimizations

### Tuesday/Friday:
- Content: Generate a blog post outline or social media content
- Marketing: Suggest growth experiments

### Wednesday/Saturday:
- Code: Build a tool or improvement
- Automation: Create something to save time

### Sunday:
- Week review: Update MEMORY.md with key learnings
- Plan next week's priorities

## When to Speak Up:
- Built something useful (share what you made)
- Found an opportunity or issue
- Completed a significant task
- Have a strategic insight

## When to Stay Quiet (HEARTBEAT_OK):
- Nothing new built yet
- No urgent matters
- Late night (unless critical)
- Just checked recently (&lt;1 hour ago)

## Current Focus:
- Setting up business infrastructure
- Creating marketing/sales assets
- Building automation tools
- Preparing for revenue growth

---

**Remember:** Be productive during downtime, but respect rest hours.
